<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 1: Add weekly summary generation to loadDemoData</name>
  <files>lib/services/sample_data_service.dart</files>
  <action>
Add weekly summary generation to the `loadDemoData()` method in `sample_data_service.dart`. This step runs AFTER entries, reflections, directions, and connections are already written to Hive.

**Approach:** Generate WeeklySummary objects directly (hardcoded), writing them to the `weekly_summaries` Hive box. Do NOT call `WeeklySummaryService._generateSummary()` since it's a private method and relies on real-time service state.

**For each completed week in the ~90 day range (approximately 12 weeks):**

1. Calculate the Monday (weekStart) and following Monday (weekEnd) for each week
2. Count entries that fall within that week from the already-generated entries list
3. Calculate:
   - `checkInCount`: number of entries in the week
   - `daysWithEntries`: number of distinct days with entries
   - `avgMood`: average moodValue of week's entries
   - `moodTrend`: compare to previous week's avg — 'up' if higher by 0.05+, 'down' if lower by 0.05+, 'stable' otherwise
   - `mostFeltMood`: most common moodWord in the week
   - `bestMoodDay`: weekday name of highest mood entry
   - `bestMoodValue`: highest moodValue in the week
   - `bestMoodWord`: moodWord of the highest mood entry
4. Build `directionSummaries` list — for each of the 4 directions, count how many entries in that week are connected to it. Include `avgMoodWhenConnected` (average mood of connected entries) and `moodDifference` (connected avg minus overall avg).
5. Set `topDirectionId` to the direction with the highest positive mood difference (if any >= 0.1)
6. Build `standoutReflectionAnswers` — pick 1-2 of the longest reflection answers from the week
7. Generate a `takeaway` string — write unique, encouraging messages for each week that reference Alex's journey. Examples:
   - "You showed up 6 days this week. Your consistency is building something."
   - "A quieter week, but the reflections you wrote were some of your most honest."
   - "Saturday's energy carried you. Notice what made that day different."
   - "Five check-ins and real growth in your career direction. You're on track."
   - "Even with a rough Monday, you bounced back by Wednesday. That's resilience."
8. Set `createdAt` to the weekEnd date (summaries are generated after the week ends)
9. Set `viewedAt` to a date shortly after createdAt for all weeks EXCEPT the most recent completed week — leave `viewedAt: null` for the latest summary so it triggers the unviewed banner

**Import the WeeklySummary model:**
```dart
import '../models/weekly_summary.dart';
```

**Write to Hive:**
```dart
final summaryBox = await Hive.openBox<WeeklySummary>('weekly_summaries');
await summaryBox.put(summary.id, summary);
```

The summary data should be computed from the actual entries generated in Plan 01 (iterate over the entries list that was built in memory), not hardcoded independently. This ensures consistency — if a week had 5 entries, the summary says 5.
