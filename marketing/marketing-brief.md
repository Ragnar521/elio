# Elio Marketing Brief

## One-Line Positioning

Elio is your everyday clarity coach: a quick mood and reflection app that helps you connect how you feel with where you want to go.

## Short Description

Elio helps busy people check in with their mood, set a simple intention, answer thoughtful reflection prompts, and notice emotional patterns over time. It is designed to feel warm, private, and practical - more like a calm daily coach than a clinical tracker or productivity tool.

## Product Promise

In 2 minutes a day, Elio helps you turn a feeling into focus.

## Target Audience

Primary users:

- Busy professionals aged 25-45.
- People who want personal growth without wellness overload.
- Users who like reflection, but do not want therapy-style language.
- People who have tried mood trackers, journals, or habit apps and felt they produced data without direction.

Likely early adopters:

- Indie makers, founders, managers, creatives, knowledge workers, and people in life transitions.
- Users already interested in journaling, self-awareness, productivity, mindfulness, or personal growth.

## Core Pain Point

People often know they feel stressed, tired, calm, or energized, but they do not always know what to do with that awareness. Existing tools either feel too clinical, too generic, or too focused on streaks and completion.

Elio gives users a gentle way to ask: What am I feeling? What matters right now? What is one small intention I can carry forward?

## Differentiators

- Mood plus direction: Elio connects emotional patterns to life areas users care about, such as health, relationships, career, growth, peace, and creativity.
- Fast daily check-ins: The main flow is built for 2-minute use, not long journaling sessions.
- Non-clinical by design: Elio avoids diagnosis, symptom framing, and therapy claims.
- Gentle insights: Weekly summaries, mood waves, day patterns, and nudges are supportive, not judgmental.
- Privacy-first: The current app stores data locally on device.
- Calm dark-first brand: Warm charcoal, soft cream, and orange create a reflective evening-friendly feel.

## Message Pillars

### 1. From Mood To Clarity

Elio is not just about logging feelings. It helps users notice what their moods might mean and turn that awareness into a small intention.

Sample copy:

- "Do not just track your mood. Understand your rhythm."
- "A 2-minute check-in for emotional clarity."
- "Turn how you feel into one small next step."

### 2. A Compass, Not A Checklist

Directions help users connect entries to life areas that matter without creating pressure to "complete" themselves.

Sample copy:

- "Life is not a checklist. Elio helps you notice your direction."
- "Connect your days to the things that matter."
- "See which parts of life lift you, drain you, or need attention."

### 3. Reflection Without Pressure

Elio supports optional reflections, skip-friendly flows, and no-guilt language.

Sample copy:

- "Reflect when you are ready."
- "Small questions. Better self-awareness."
- "No pressure, no streak shame, no clinical labels."

### 4. Useful Patterns, Not Data Overload

Elio turns mood entries into weekly summaries, mood waves, day patterns, and simple insights.

Sample copy:

- "See your emotional rhythm without drowning in charts."
- "Weekly insights that feel human."
- "Notice patterns. Keep what helps. Adjust what does not."

## Tone Of Voice

Elio sounds:

- Warm, calm, and clear.
- Supportive but not overly sweet.
- Practical but not corporate.
- Reflective but not spiritual-heavy.
- Encouraging without telling users what they "should" do.

Use:

- "check in"
- "capture how you feel"
- "quick reflection"
- "emotional rhythm"
- "things that matter"
- "life directions"
- "gentle insight"

Avoid:

- "mental health treatment"
- "therapy"
- "diagnose"
- "symptoms"
- "fix yourself"
- "optimize your life"
- "hack your mood"
- "maximize productivity"

## Claims Guardrails

Safe claims:

- "Helps you reflect on your mood."
- "Helps you notice patterns over time."
- "Designed for quick daily check-ins."
- "Supports intention setting and reflection."
- "Stores data locally on device" if this remains true at launch.

Avoid claims:

- "Improves mental health."
- "Reduces anxiety/depression."
- "Treats stress."
- "AI therapist."
- "Scientifically proven to make you happier."

## Suggested Taglines

- Your everyday clarity coach.
- From feeling to focus.
- Clarity in 2 minutes.
- Connect emotions to action.
- A calmer way to understand your day.
- Mood tracking with direction.

## Brand Boilerplate

Elio is a mood and clarity app for people who want a simple daily way to understand how they feel and stay connected to what matters. Through quick mood check-ins, intentions, reflections, life directions, and gentle weekly insights, Elio helps users build self-awareness without pressure, clinical language, or data overload.
