# Elio Marketing Pack

Prepared for the marketing team from local project files and the Elio Notion workspace.

## Files

- `marketing-brief.md` - positioning, audience, message pillars, tone, proof points, and claims guardrails.
- `launch-campaign-topics.md` - launch campaign structure, angles, channels, content topics, and a simple 4-week rollout.
- `store-description.md` - App Store / Google Play listing copy, short descriptions, keywords, screenshot captions, and release notes.
- `social-posts.md` - ready-to-adapt launch posts for LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Instagram, TikTok/Reels, Reddit, Product Hunt, and email.

## Source Notes

This pack uses these product truths:

- Elio is an everyday clarity coach for quick mood check-ins, intentions, goal-aware reflections, and life directions.
- Elio is not a clinical mental health, therapy, meditation, or generic habit-tracking app.
- Core promise: help users connect how they feel with what matters to them, what showed up today, what moved forward, and what got in the way.
- Tone: warm, clear, supportive, non-judgmental, and respectful of busy lives.
- Product status in the repo: v2.1 demo mode shipped; current direction work adds multi-goal check-ins, optional per-goal small steps/blockers/support notes, goal-specific reflection prompts, direction detail editing, and insight language that separates presence from progress.
